01. Bali

Myriads of beaches and volcanic peaks have made this place a paradise for trekkers and surfers. Besides the legendary swells, rice terraces, waterfalls, traditional villages etc.Bali is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, ranging from white sand to black sand, from calm to surf-friendly, from secluded to lively. You can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, snorkeling, diving, surfing, or just relaxing on the beach with your partner.

A stunning architecture, fancy hotels, shopping festivals, majestic skyscrapers, glittering skylines, and giant shopping malls, Dubai is truly a paradise for scores of tourists from around the world. Known to be one of the most progressive regions in the Middle East region.
